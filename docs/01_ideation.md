# 01 — Project Ideation

## Project Name
HealSound — AI-Powered Music Genre Classification for 
Health and Social Wellness

## Problem Statement
Music has clinically proven effects on human mental and 
physical health, yet no existing platform uses AI-based 
genre classification to deliver personalised therapeutic 
music or monitor community acoustic wellness in real time. 
Manual music curation for therapy is slow, inconsistent, 
and not scalable.

## Target Users
- Patients experiencing depression, chronic pain, insomnia
- Therapists and counsellors needing data-driven tools
- City planners and NGOs monitoring urban noise stress
- Students and working professionals under daily stress

## What Makes It Unique
- First system combining genre classification with a 
  passive depression early-warning tracker
- Live city noise wellness map powered by the same CNN
- Carnatic raga therapeutic mapping — an India-first feature
- Single CNN engine serving both health and social missions

## Real-World Impact
- Early mental health intervention through passive monitoring
- Scalable to hospitals and schools without extra hardware
- Raises noise pollution awareness in Indian urban areas
