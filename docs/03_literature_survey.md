# 03 — Literature Survey

## Papers Reviewed

### 1. Music Genre Classification using CNN
- Authors: Nitin Choudhury et al.
- Key finding: CNN on spectrograms achieves 92.65% on GTZAN
- Gap: No health application layer

### 2. Deep Content-Based Music Recommendation
- Authors: Oord et al. (2013)
- Key finding: CNN learns audio features without manual tags
- Gap: Recommendation only, no therapeutic mapping

### 3. Music and Health — What Kind of Music Helps?
- Authors: Thoma et al.
- Key finding: Slow tempo, low pitch music reduces cortisol
- Gap: No automated genre-based prescription system

### 4. Urban Sound Classification
- Authors: Salamon et al.
- Key finding: CNN classifies 10 urban sound categories
- Gap: No city wellness mapping output

### 5. Predicting Depression from Smartphone Data
- Authors: Saeb et al.
- Key finding: Passive phone data predicts depression risk
- Gap: Music listening patterns not included as a signal

## Research Gap This Project Fills
No existing system combines music genre AI with 
passive depression monitoring AND urban acoustic 
wellness mapping in a single deployable platform.
